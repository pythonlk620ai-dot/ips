🚀 Version 3.0 Launch

What's New in Version 3.0?

Our latest release includes over 50 improvements and new features based on your feedback. Here are the highlights:

🔒 New AI-Powered Background Removal

Our new AI algorithm provides pixel-perfect background removal in seconds. Simply select your subject and let our technology handle the rest!

🎨 Enhanced Filter Library

We've added 20+ new filters including cinematic looks, vintage effects, and artistic styles. All filters are now non-destructive and can be adjusted in real-time.

⚡ Fast Performance Improvements

Editor loads 3x faster with improved rendering engine. Smooth performance even with large, complex projects with multiple layers.

📱 New Mobile Optimization

Full touch support for tablets and mobile devices. All tools are now optimized for touch interaction with gesture controls.

💾 New Auto-Save Feature

Never lose your work again! Your projects are automatically saved every 30 seconds and can be restored even if you accidentally close the browser.

🖼️ Enhanced Export Options

Export in 10+ formats including WebP, HEIC, and advanced PNG options. Control quality, compression, and metadata with ease.
