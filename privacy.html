Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

At Img-PS, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online image editor.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Important: Img-PS is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect or store:

  • Your images or photos
  • Your editing history or changes
  • Any personal information from your editing sessions
  • Files you upload or create

All processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to make the editor work properly. These cookies are necessary for the functionality of our service and do not track you across the web.

We also use third-party advertising cookies (Google AdSense) to display relevant ads. You can opt out of these cookies through your browser settings or the Google AdSense privacy center.

4. Third-Party Services

Img-PS may use third-party services that have access to limited information necessary to provide the service. These services include:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Analytics services: To understand how our service is used (anonymously)

These third parties have strict privacy policies and are prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
    • >Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@img-ps.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
