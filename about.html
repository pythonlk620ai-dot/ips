About Us

Learn more about Img-PS and our mission

Who We Are

Img-PS is a powerful online image editor that brings professional-grade photo editing capabilities directly to your browser. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality image editing tools without the need for expensive software or downloads.

Founded with a vision to make photo editing accessible to everyone, we've created a platform that combines the power of desktop applications with the convenience of web-based tools. Our editor runs entirely in your browser, ensuring your privacy and security while providing an exceptional editing experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower users with powerful, intuitive, and completely free image editing tools. We strive to:

  • Provide professional-grade editing capabilities
  • Ensure complete privacy and security
  • Make photo editing accessible to everyone
  • Deliver a seamless user experience

Our Features

Img-PS offers a comprehensive suite of tools including:

  • Layer-based editing
  • Advanced filters and effects
  • Text and drawing tools
  • Background removal
  • Image resizing and cropping
  • Color adjustment tools
