Getting Started

Welcome to Img-PS! Our online image editor is designed to be intuitive and powerful. Here's how to get started:

  • Upload an image: Click "File" → "Open" or drag and drop your image onto the editor
  • Explore tools: Use the left sidebar for editing tools and right sidebar for layers and properties
  • Apply effects: Try filters, adjustments, and other effects from the menu
  • Save your work: Click "File" → "Save As" to download your edited image

Features Guide

Layers

Layers allow you to work on different parts of your image independently. You can add text, images, or adjustments as separate layers.

Filters

Apply beautiful filters to enhance your photos. Browse through categories like Blur, Color, Distort, and more.

Text Tools

Add text to your images with customizable fonts, sizes, colors, and effects. You can rotate, scale, and position text precisely.

Troubleshooting

Editor not loading?

Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari) and that JavaScript is enabled.

Can't upload images?

Check that your image file is in a supported format (JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP) and isn't too large (under 10MB recommended).

Performance issues?

Try closing other browser tabs, reducing image size, or using simpler effects. The editor works best with modern hardware.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Img-PS really free?

Yes! Img-PS is completely free to use. No hidden fees, no subscriptions required.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use Img-PS without creating an account. All editing happens in your browser.

Are my images saved on your servers?

No, your images never leave your device. Everything is processed in your browser for maximum privacy.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP formats for both opening and saving images.
